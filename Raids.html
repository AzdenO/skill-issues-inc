Raids and Dungeons

Raids. Dungeons. The pinnacle of teamwork and fireteam coordination. Legends have been forged and weakness purged. Raids and Dungeons (RAD) offer an entirely unique experience to guardians who can prove their strength.

Here at the company, we run raids nearly every day of the week, and anyone can create a sign-up card in the discord server. Haven`t done a certain raid? Ask for teachers. Want to go after a raid title? There`s plenty of dedicated raiders who will rise to the challenge. We want newcomers to dive head first into these end-game activities; the more people who know a raid, the more often it can be ran.

Raid Rules

I know, very boring. But it`s gotta be done, in the interest of smoothness, coordination and making sure a good time, stays a good time. The following are a list of rules we want all raiders and server users to be wary of:

  • If you have signed up to a raid card. You must arrive at the start time on the card. If you are going to be late, alert the rest of the team at the earliest opportunity
  • If you are signing up to a raid you have not done before, or have little experience, check with those on the card that there is enough people with amble experience to guide you through
  • Absolutely NO SKILL TOXICITY. If you have joined a casual run, or a run where people are learning, do not expect smoothness or fast clears. If you are wanting runs such as those then create a card and specify such in the cards description
  • The alternate option on a raid card (denoted by a "?") is for signalling you will be available at the start time of the raid and can fill a spot if needed. There is no other use for this option
  • If you would like to do a raid you do not know, either ask for sherpas who will create a card for you, or create a card yourself but the description on the card must denote you have no/little experience and sherpas are required
  • This should go without saying but for some, it must be said. NO BULLYING. We have a ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY for any kind of verbal abuse. While insulting each other can be seen as banter, never let it go to far, and never outright use slurs of any kind. Confirmed cases of such will lead to an immediate ban
